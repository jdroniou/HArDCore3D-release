\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Base class for D\+OF spaces\+: functions to access local D\+O\+Fs (organised from the smallest dimension to the largest) associated with each geometric entity. 

In a cell T, for example, the D\+O\+Fs are presented in the order\+: D\+O\+Fs of vertices of T, D\+O\+Fs of edges of T, D\+O\+Fs of faces of T, D\+O\+Fs of T.
