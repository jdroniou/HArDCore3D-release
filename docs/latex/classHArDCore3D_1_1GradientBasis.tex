\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\subsubsection*{template$<$typename Basis\+Type$>$\newline
class HAr\+DCore3\+D\+::\+Gradient\+Basis$<$ Basis\+Type $>$}

Basis for the space of gradients of polynomials. 

To construct a basis of G$^\wedge$k, this assumes that the scalar basis it is constructed from is an ancestor basis of P$^\wedge$\{k+1\}/\+P$^\wedge$0, space of polynomials of degree k+1 without trivial polynomial with zero gradient (e.\+g. polynomials with zero average, or a hierarchical basis of P$^\wedge$\{k+1\} in which we have removed the first, constant, polynomial). This can also be used to create a family of gradients (not necessarily linearly independent, if the ancestor basis is not a basis of P$^\wedge$\{k+1\}/\+P$^\wedge$0)
