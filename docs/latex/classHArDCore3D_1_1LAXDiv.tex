\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Discrete Lie algebra valued Hdiv space\+: local operators, L2 product and global interpolator. 

Each DOF in the \mbox{\hyperlink{classHArDCore3D_1_1XDiv}{XDiv}} space corresponds to the dimension of the Lie algebra number of DOFs in the \mbox{\hyperlink{classHArDCore3D_1_1LAXDiv}{LAXDiv}} space. These DOFs are stored consecutively with overall ordering given by \mbox{\hyperlink{classHArDCore3D_1_1XDiv}{XDiv}}; they represent and are locally ordered by the basis in the \mbox{\hyperlink{classHArDCore3D_1_1LieAlgebra}{Lie\+Algebra}}
