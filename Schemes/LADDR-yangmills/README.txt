Directory: HArDCore3D/Schemes/LADDR-yangmills

The DDR scheme implemented here is designed and analysed in

  A polyhedral discrete de Rham numerical scheme for the Yang–Mills equations. 
  J. Droniou, T. A. Oliynyk, and J. J. Qian, J. Comput. Phys. 26p, 2023.
  doi: 10.1016/j.jcp.2023.111955. url: https://arxiv.org/abs/2208.12009.
  
The SDDR scheme is designed and analysed in
  
  Two arbitrary-order constraint-preserving schemes for the Yang–Mills equations on polyhedral meshes. 
  J. Droniou and J. J. Qian, 21p, 2023.

If you use this code for a scientific publication, please mention the above articles.
