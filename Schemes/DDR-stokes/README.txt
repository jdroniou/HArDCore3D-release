Directory: HArDCore3D/Schemes/DDR-stokes

The DDR scheme implemented here is designed and analysed in

  Arbitrary-order pressure-robust DDR and VEM methods for the Stokes problem on polyhedral meshes. 
  L. Beirao da Veiga, F. Dassi, D. A. Di Pietro, and J. Droniou.
  Comput. Methods Appl. Mech. Engrg. 397, Paper No. 115061, 2022. 
  doi: 10.1016/j.cma.2022.115061. url: https://arxiv.org/abs/2112.09750.
  
  An arbitrary-order discrete de Rham complex on polyhedral meshes: Exactness, Poincaré inequalities, and consistency. 
  D. A. Di Pietro and J. Droniou. Found. Comput. Math. 80p, 2021. 
  doi: 10.1007/s10208-021-09542-8. url: https://arxiv.org/abs/2101.04940.

See also, for the serendipity version:

  Homological- and analytical-preserving serendipity framework for polytopal complexes, with application to the DDR method. 
  D. A. Di Pietro and J. Droniou, M2AN Math. Model. Numer. Anal. 57, pp. 191–225, 1 2023. 
  doi: 10.1051/m2an/2022067. url: https://arxiv.org/abs/2203.02939.
